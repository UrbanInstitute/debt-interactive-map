The Geography of Debt

Credit can be a lifeline during emergencies and a bridge to education and homeownership. But not all debt starts out as credit, and many people struggle with debt burdens. These burdens can ripple through families and communities and can exacerbate wealth inequality. This map shows the geography of debt in America. How do your county and state compare?
